# Coverage Area

KiriminAja supports a unified coverage mapping combining all logistics partners into a single, standardized area reference

Our service covers a wide range of areas, ensuring your parcels reach their destinations swiftly and securely. Whether you prefer integrating through our user-friendly API for automated processes or using manual mapping for more personalized control, we offer flexible options to meet your specific needs.

## Unified Coverage Mapping

We use a unified coverage mapping system that consolidates all areas served by every logistics partner we work with into a single, standardized reference. This means you don't need to check each logistics provider's coverage separately — our mapping handles it for you.

### How It Works

- Single data source — All supported areas from our partnered logistics providers are merged into one mapping file.
- Standardized area codes — Each area is assigned a consistent identifier, regardless of which logistics partner serves it.
- Regular updates — The mapping is updated periodically to reflect new areas and logistics partner expansions.
